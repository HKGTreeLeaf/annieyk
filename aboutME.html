About Me


Hello, I am Yuen-Ki Wong, a third-year Bachelor of Fine Arts student at the Open University of Hong Kong, majoring in Animation and Visual Effects. Looking for animation related internship and freelance.

I am interested in animation art, especially in creating 2-D and 3-D computer animation, character design, background design, colour script, colour grading and digital visual effects.

As an animator, the favourite part in making animation is the moment I share my work with the audience, knowing that they find my animation enjoyable and pleasurable to watch.

I hope I could apply my knowledge to workplaces related to animation-making and hence, Ultimately, with the experiences gained in internship and freelance, I wish to contribute to society upon graduation.



Skills: Photoshop, illustration, After Effects, Premiere Pro, Flash, Maya, Mudbox, PFTrack, Matchmover
